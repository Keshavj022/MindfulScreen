Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 21, 2025 | Version 1.0

Your Privacy Matters: MindfulScreen is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Data We Collect

Account Information

  • Name, email address, password (encrypted)
  • Age, gender, occupation, location (optional demographics)
  • Personality quiz responses

Screen Recording Data

  • Screenshots captured during your recording sessions
  • Metadata: timestamp, detected app, content type
  • AI-generated analysis: sentiment, wellness scores
  • Extracted text and language detection

Technical Data

  • IP address, browser type, device information
  • Login timestamps and session activity
  • Audit logs for security purposes

2. How We Protect Your Data

End-to-End Encryption: All captured frames are encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption
Secure Password Storage: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with salt
HTTPS Only: All data transmission uses TLS/SSL encryption
Local Storage: All data is stored locally on secure servers, not shared with third parties
Secure Deletion: Deleted files are overwritten before removal

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Personalization: To provide personalized wellness insights and recommendations
  • Analysis: To analyze your screen time patterns using OpenAI's GPT-4 Vision
  • Improvements: To improve our AI models and service quality (anonymized data only)
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Communications: To send important service updates (never spam)

4. Data Sharing

We DO NOT sell your personal data to anyone.

We only share data with:

  • OpenAI: Screen frames are sent to OpenAI's API for analysis (see their privacy policy)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all your data
  • Export: Download your data in JSON format
  • Delete: Permanently delete your account and all associated data
  • Correct: Update or correct your personal information
  • Withdraw Consent: Stop screen recording at any time
  • Object: Opt out of specific data processing activities

Manage Your Data

6. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while your account is active
  • Auto-Deletion: Frames older than 30 days are automatically deleted (configurable)
  • Account Deletion: All data permanently deleted within 72 hours of account deletion
  • Audit Logs: Retained for 90 days for security purposes

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies for:

  • Session management and authentication
  • Security (CSRF protection)
  • Remembering your preferences

We do NOT use tracking or advertising cookies.

8. Children's Privacy

MindfulScreen is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. International Users

Your data may be processed in servers located in different countries. By using MindfulScreen, you consent to this transfer while we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. We will notify you of significant changes via email or a prominent notice on our service. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
